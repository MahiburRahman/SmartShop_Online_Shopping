About Us

Smart Shop(SS) is the largest laptop Distributor in Bangladesh. For SS, its customers are not only the most important stakeholders; rather they are regarded as the most valued partners as well. SS is pledge bound to ensure the best services and solutions to its customers, offering a rewarding experience in purchasing and owning a SS laptop. Customers' satisfaction and loyalty oblige SS for furthering efforts and deliver the largest array of IT products nationwide.

Smart Shop was created in 2014 by a RUETIAN . Since the company's inception, SS business executives and engineers have been leaders in the market in selling, distributing and providing solution for technology products. Today, with more than 750 employees nationwide, this tradition of marketing innovation continues with industry-leading products and solutions in the company's core business areas.
